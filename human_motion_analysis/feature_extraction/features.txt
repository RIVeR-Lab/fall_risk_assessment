Features:
1: total duration of the test (from "go" command until person sits)
2: total number of steps
3: average of the step distance in x axes
4: average of the step distance in y axes
5: average of the step distance in z axes (how much person is lifting his food from ground)
6: average of the elbow distance x axes


7: average knee angle (R/L)
8: average hip angle (R/L)
9: average elbow angle (R/L)


frame transforms:
1: left_foot -> right_foot
2: right_wrist -> neck
3: left_wrist -> neck
